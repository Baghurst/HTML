Where is Wales within the UK?

Wales is one of the four countries that make up the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
We are located on an island called Great Britain, which we share with Scotland and England.

Wales has its own devolved government and its own parliament (the Senedd),
although some political decisions are still made by the UK Government,
like matters of crime and policing, defence, and foreign affairs.
People in Wales travel on British passports,
like all citizens who live within the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.
