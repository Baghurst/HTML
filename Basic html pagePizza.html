What is Real Italian Pizza?

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat,
leavened bread dough baked with toppings,
most commonly tomato sauce and cheese, and sometimes other ingredients like vegetables, meat, and herbs.
It originated in Naples, Italy,
and has evolved from a simple flatbread into a diverse and globally popular food,
baked at high temperatures and often served hot.

Classic Neopolitan Style Pizza.
